02 — METHODOLOGY

How We Work

A fixed sequence, applied to every engagement. Each stage produces a concrete artifact before the next begins — nothing moves forward on assumption.

01 / DISCOVER

Map the workflow

We embed with your operations team to trace how work, money, and information actually move — not how the org chart says they should.

→ Week 1 · Workflow map + bottleneck audit

02 / DESIGN

Architect the system

We design the system architecture around the bottleneck, not the technology — specifying data models, decision points, and integration boundaries.

→ Weeks 3–5 · Deployed working system

Once the system proves out on one site or team, we harden it for multi-site rollout and hand over the operating playbook.

→ Week 6+ · Rollout plan + case study
